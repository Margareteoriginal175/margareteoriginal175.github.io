## 🔌 Connecting Your Device

Successful recovery requires a stable connection between your phone and your computer. Follow these steps to prepare your Android device:

1. Enable USB Debugging in your phone settings.
2. Navigate to Settings and select About Phone.
3. Tap the Build Number seven times to unlock Developer Options.
4. Return to the main Settings menu and open Developer Options.
5. Toggle the USB Debugging switch to the On position.
6. Connect your phone to your computer using your USB cable.
7. Confirm the connection prompt that appears on your phone screen to allow data access.

## 🛡️ Best Practices for Recovery

Speed influences the success of your recovery efforts. If you accidentally delete a file, follow these steps to increase the chances of a successful restoration:

* Stop using the device immediately to prevent overwriting deleted data.
* Avoid installing new applications or taking photos after data loss occurs.
* Keep your phone connected to a stable power source during the scan.
* Ensure your computer remains awake during the recovery process to prevent connection drops.
* Save the recovered data to your computer hard drive rather than back to the phone internal storage to avoid potential conflicts.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What if the software fails to detect my phone?
Verify that your USB cable is functional and securely plugged into a high-speed port on your computer. Check that USB Debugging remains enabled and that you granted permission on the phone screen.

Does this tool work if the screen is broken?
If the computer recognizes the device and you can enable USB debugging, the software can pull information from the drive. Physical damage to the screen does not always prevent data access.

Will my current phone data get deleted during recovery?
The software performs a read-only operation. It does not alter, delete, or modify existing data on your phone during the scan.

Is my data secure?
The software runs locally on your Windows machine. It does not upload your personal files to any third-party servers. All recovery happens offline.

Can I recover files from an SD card?
Yes, the software supports external storage recovery. Connect your SD card via a card reader if the phone does not respond, or scan the phone directly if it supports memory card storage.
